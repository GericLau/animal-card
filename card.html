Poodle

The poodle is a group of formal dog breeds which is adorable.

  • Other Names: Caniche
  • Origin: Uncertain - France
  • Size: Standard, Miniature, and Toy
  • Color: white, black, brown, parti, silver

The poodle is an active, intelligent and elegant dog, squarely built, and well proportioned. To ensure the desirable squarely built appearance, the length of body measured from the breastbone to the point of the rump approximates the height from the highest point of the shoulders to the ground.
